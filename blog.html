Diplomas
There’s a lot to learn at Trek University so we’ve broken it down into a few manageable chunks. We start with seven schools and then break those into different subjects. If you complete a school you have the opportunity to download a diploma to show off to your friends or prove to your boss that you know your stuff. Here’s how it breaks out: Finish all the modules in any of these 5 areas of knowledge and receive your diploma.
  • Road = Road Scholar Diploma
  • Mountain = Dirt Scientist Diploma
  • City = City Savant Diploma
  • Bontrager = Bontrager Philosopher Diploma
  • Retail = Retail Virtuoso Diploma
Remember, from time to time when you earn a diploma (or reach other such milestones) you’ll have the opportunity for a great deal on bikes or frames that you can’t get anywhere else. So read your email and keep up to date on the news section of Trek University!
